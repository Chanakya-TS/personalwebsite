FirefightAR

3rd — Georgia Tech Snap Challenge • Lens Studio · YOLOv5 · Firebase

Demo video

Problem

Firefighters face low‑visibility and rapidly changing environments. Quick identification of hazards, exits, and victims can save lives.

Solution

An AR experience for Snap Spectacles that highlights hazards and victims in real‑time and guides users to exits with contextual overlays.

Technical details

  • YOLOv5 model tuned for smoke/low‑light conditions.
  • Lens Studio rendering with on‑device inference constraints.
  • Realtime data sync and event logging with Firebase.

Outcome

  • Placed 3rd in the Georgia Tech Snap Challenge.
  • Demonstrated feasibility for assisting first responders.
